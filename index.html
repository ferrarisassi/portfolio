Joene e o Castelo Ilusionista

Developed for the PodQuestJam 2021. Embark on this challenge and help Joene discover what happened to her family's castle! In the Illusionist Castle, nothing is what it seems to be. Taken by a strange curse, the castle that used to be cozy and welcoming is now dark and menacing. Explore its rooms, unravel its mysteries, and use the Light of Truth to free the castle from the Curse!

Main Contributions:

  • Worked as level designer and programmer

Of a Feather

Developed for the Cultural-Cross Impact Gamejam 2022. You play as a crow who has lost its feathers and cannot fly. In order to regain your ability to fly, you must talk to people and listen to their stories. You will be given a feather in learning about their experiences and doing small quests for them. When you gather enough feathers, you can fly away and win the game.

Main Contributions:

  • Designed and implemented the dialogue system and its UI
  • Designed and implemented the quest system
